Insurance & Payment Options

Insurance Coverage

While we don't currently accept insurance directly, many patients successfully use their HSA/FSA accounts for our services.

  • HSA/FSA Eligible: All consultation fees
  • Insurance Rx: Medications may be covered by your prescription plan
  • Superbills: Detailed receipts for insurance reimbursement
  • Tax Deductible: Medical expenses may be deductible

Payment Methods

We offer flexible payment options to make treatment accessible.

  • Credit/Debit Cards: All major cards accepted
  • HSA/FSA Cards: Direct payment from health accounts
  • Monthly Billing: Automatic recurring payments
  • Flexible Scheduling: Pause treatment when needed
