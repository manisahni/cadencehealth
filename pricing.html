Insurance & Payment Options

Insurance Coverage

While we don't currently accept insurance directly, many patients successfully use their HSA/FSA accounts for our services.

  • HSA/FSA Eligible: All consultation fees
  • Insurance Rx: Medications may be covered by your prescription plan
  • Superbills: Detailed receipts for insurance reimbursement
