Why GLP-1 Therapy Works

GLP-1 (Glucagon-like peptide-1) medications work by mimicking a hormone your body naturally produces to regulate blood sugar and appetite.

How It Works

  • Slows gastric emptying, helping you feel full longer
  • Reduces appetite and food cravings
  • Regulates blood sugar levels
  • Improves insulin sensitivity

Clinical Results

  • 15-22% average weight loss in clinical trials
  • Improved cardiovascular health markers
  • Better blood sugar control
  • Reduced risk of diabetes complications
